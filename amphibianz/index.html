Who We Are

Explore Kuttanad with Amphibianz

Immerse yourself in the tranquillity of nature, paddle through scenic waterways, and experience the thrill of adventure with our premier kayaking services. Whether you're a seasoned paddler seeking a new challenge or a first-timer eager to dip your paddle into the world of kayaking, we have something special for everyone. The unique lifestyle makes the village more interesting. We invite everyone to experience the circle of life in Kuttanad, a small village in the Alappuzha, Kerala in India.

  • People always need spaces to explore their happiness at its peak and make it a memory. The project AMPHIBIANZ is for making Kuttanad a perfect space for this. Kuttanad is a beautiful place to visit & spend time. An isolated place where the inmates and the culture are still hanging in there without any change but can adapt all kinds of moderation to their hearts, which makes the village unique. AMPHIBIANZ will be the exact option for exaggeration, homely attire and enthusiastic relationships with a free mind. We are mainly focusing on the happiness of guest, who came to ease out their mind. It includes cycling, village area explorations & boating during all weather conditions to experience real village life.
